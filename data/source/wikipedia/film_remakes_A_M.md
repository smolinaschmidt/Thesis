From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a **list of film remakes**. Excluded in this list are films that are based on the same source material. For example, the 1962 version of _Mutiny on the Bounty_ is not a remake of the 1935 film; both are based on the novel _Mutiny on the Bounty_. Reboots are also omitted.

This list is ordered by the title of the original film, inasmuch as there can be multiple remakes.
